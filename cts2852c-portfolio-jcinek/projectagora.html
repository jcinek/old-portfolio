    Project: Agora

    Agora is a live chatroom site that I designed and created myself for my college Web Authoring II class. It features 11 chatrooms in total, including a general chat, music chat, movies chat, as well as a private VIP chatroom. Agora was founded on the principles of acceptance, sharing new ideas, and people just having friendly conversation.
