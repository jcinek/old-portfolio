    Project: VOID

    This video game cover art is my first ever completed Photoshop project. Though the game "VOID" does not actually exist, I formed my own idea of what a space exploration RPG made by myself might look like, and created this cover art around that concept.

    This project consists of approximately 20 physical layers, along with numerous effects layers and blending effects. Can you see something like this on the cover of your next project? Be sure to visit my Contact page to get in game.
